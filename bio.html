My Muay Thai Story

Semi-Pro Muay Thai Fighter and Instructor

Bio

Elizabeth, or Liz as she prefers to be called, was born in Belleville, Canada in 1991. From a young age, she was active on and off in sports but was neither
passionate nor competitive. Swimming, hockey, volleyball--none of these seemed to light up her spirit or inspire her to excel. School was much the same with only
and the occasional Physical Education class sparking her interest and demanding her energy. Often a frustrating experience, school was often a glorified daycare
for Liz and she acted out and became complacent with life. After finally earning her high school diploma, Liz moved in with a hostile boyfriend and the relationship
took an enormous mental toll on her. One day, while riding a bus home, she saw the sign that would change her life. In simple, blocky black letters it seemed to shout
to her BEGINNER KICKBOXING STARTS APRIL 4 which became a reimaging of my whole self-perception and a guiding light out of a difficult time in my life.

Since 2010, Liz has been practicing Muay Thai primarily under the the instruction of Kelly Westerlund, owner and head coach of Kwest Muay Thai in Winnipeg, Canada.

Teaching and Philosophy

I have been teaching beginner Muay Thai at the Colosseum in Aarhus, Denmark since Summer of 2021 and have introduced fighting to many curious students.
"My philosophy about kickboxing classes is it should be 60% fun and 40% grind. No one wants to come to a class and feel like their in military basic training.
But even though I make it fun, everyone leaves drenched in sweat."
Currently, I teach beginner Muay Thai at the Colosseum, running both a non-profit class and my own private lessons for women.
These ladies' classes take place in the morning, an often neglected part of the day for combat sports clubs, on Mondays and Wednesdays, and a catch-all
class at 11:30 am on Sundays. For those curious about the private lessons and can't make it for evening classes, come join the facebook group!
You can also check out the schedule page for exact times.
